About Maclink

Maclink is a decentralized, blockchain- and based online earning application that operates on the usdt network.

It functions as a smart contract program allowing participants to earn dollars through a peer-to-peer matrix system. Users join by paying an entry fee in dollars (USDT), which grants them access to the matrix. Participants can earn through a referral program by inviting others to join the platform. As new members join under their referral link, they can earn commissions based on the contributions made by these new members. Maclink operates on a self-executing smart contract, ensuring transparency and immutability of transactions.
